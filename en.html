Who stands beside you

Two decades where everything is at stake.

I learned my profession where mistakes are not an option: in the intensive care units and anaesthesia departments of Germany's major hospitals. For more than twenty years I cared for people in the most critical moments of their lives — and learned to recognise a situation early, before it becomes serious.

Later I took on responsibility: as owner and director of nursing of three outpatient care services in the upscale segment in Düsseldorf. I know care from both sides — from the bedside to running a business: the hospitals, the processes, the right people to call.

Across all these years I saw one thing again and again: how many crises and hospital stays could have been avoided if someone had looked more closely, sooner. That is exactly why I now bring Sensora Care — modern 24/7 vital-sign early detection combined with my personal nursing judgement.

I grew up in Spain and in Germany — at home in both worlds. I know both languages, both cultures and both healthcare systems from my own experience. In Mallorca this makes all the difference: I know how things work here, who the right people are, and how to reach the goal safely — without detours and without a language barrier.

Today I place this experience at the service of a small number of private clients in Mallorca. Not as an agency. Not as a middleman. Me, personally.

Your questions

Everything you may want to know — before you reach out.

Am I tied to a contract or minimum term?+

No. The first conversation is free of charge and without obligation. We shape the collaboration around your situation — from support for a single hospital stay to ongoing care.

What does it cost?+

That depends on the scope — accompanying you to one appointment is different from continuous 24/7 support. After our first conversation you receive a clear, transparent proposal. No hidden costs, no obligation.

How quickly can I reach you?+

My clients reach me directly — evenings and weekends included. I usually respond to new enquiries within 24 hours. Important: in an acute medical emergency, always call 112 first.

Do you replace my doctor?+

No — and that is a mark of quality. I work alongside your doctors, not in their place. My role is coordination, accompaniment and nursing expertise. Medical decisions are always made by your treating physicians — I make sure you understand those decisions and can ask the right questions.

I'm healthy — when is the right time?+

Right now. The best collaboration begins before anything happens: we organise your records, your medication and your contacts — so that in an emergency everything is ready and nobody has to search.

How confidential is our collaboration?+

Absolutely confidential. No data shared, no names mentioned, no references without your explicit consent. Discretion is not an extra service — it is the foundation.
