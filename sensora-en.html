What is Sensora Care

Not more data. Better orientation.

Sensora is an intelligent information and prioritisation layer for safer care: wearable signals, context and prioritisation logic are combined so that relevant changes should become visible earlier — before they escalate.

What matters is not the technology alone. It is the connection with a person who can interpret the signals: over more than twenty years in intensive care I learned to recognise developments before they become serious. Sensora gives that experience a head start.

This combination — cutting-edge care-intelligence technology and personal nursing companionship — you will not find twice in Mallorca.

Your questions

What you should know about Sensora Care.

Does Sensora Care replace the emergency services?+

No. Sensora Care is an orientation and early-detection system, not an emergency system. In an acute medical emergency, always call 112 first. Sensora helps you see developments earlier — it does not replace the emergency services.

Too complicated for my parents?+

No — that is the whole point. Only a wearable is worn, nothing more. I handle the set-up in person, explain everything calmly and remain the point of contact for every question. No one has to understand technology to benefit from it.

Who sees the data?+

Only the people you designate — role-based and traceable. No sharing with third parties, no use for other purposes. Discretion is my foundation, not an add-on.

Does Sensora replace the doctor or nursing care?+

No. Sensora Care provides orientation — diagnoses and treatment decisions are made exclusively by the treating physicians. I make sure that relevant observations reach the right people earlier.

What does Sensora Care cost?+

It depends on the scope of the support. After the first conversation you receive a clear, transparent proposal — with no hidden costs and no obligation.
